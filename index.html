Background

Bensley Media is a full-service video production agency based in Adelaide, South Australia.

Led by Creative Director Ben Crossley, we take a hands-on, collaborative approach to every project. From concept development and filming through to editing and final delivery, Ben personally oversees each stage to ensure your story is told with clarity, creativity, and impact.

We work closely with a trusted network of industry professionals, including producers, presenters, camera operators, marketing specialists, and photographers, allowing us to scale our team to suit the unique needs of every production.

Over the years, Bensley Media has proudly partnered with clients across a wide range of industries, including local and state government, education, construction, hospitality, entertainment, non-profits, defence, sport and recreation, and the SA Film Corporation.
